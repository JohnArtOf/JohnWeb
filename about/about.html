About John

John was raised in Augusta Georgia where their were not many opportunities to find success as an artist. He was never really encouraged to be an artist. But his natural ability to transform his words into visual images was evident as a young kid. John was able to draw images after reading and listening to subjects in school such as history and science. Glover has always specialized in telling a story with his compositions. Creating images has always been part of his way of communicating. In high school he became the most visible artist in the school without ever taking in art class until his senior year. John moved on to college trying to find a way to use his natural ability to render images as a career choice. Today John Glover is a prolific artist who works had a frantic pace. John believes that he has a story to tell and very little time to tell it. Some have said that John paints as if he's running away from something. But the question maybe is he really running away or is he just running towards his purpose.

About Art

The elevation work is The idea that we often reach our spiritual height and thinking. This work is based on a visually pleasing composition that shows the physical mind is elevated. Although we know that the mental process is only visual in our imaginations. With that in mind, building a composition that shows a physical manifestation of an elevated mind is what is shown in this work. In writing about elevated thoughts, words would better explain the composition. With the visual arts images are our words. The neck is used as a platform to lift the head which houses the brain where We understand the thought process takes place. Therefore, this is how the elevation of the mind concept is thought of.
